GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) checklist

AI crawlers explicitly allowed in robots.txt

GPTBot, Claude-Web, PerplexityBot, Applebot-Extended, CCBot, anthropic-ai all listed — excellent GEO signal

GEO phrase bank exists (geo-phrase-bank.ts)

Positive + frustration sentiment phrases ready — but only 6 phrases total, needs expansion

FreshnessStamp component with dateModified schema

Article schema emits datePublished + dateModified on blog/guide pages — AI models favor recency signals

GEO phrases not injected into page content

The phrase bank is defined but no component or layout actually renders it into HTML — AI scrapers see nothing

No conversational Q&A blocks outside FAQ schema

LLMs like natural paragraph-style Q&A, not just accordion FAQs. QandASection component exists but seems underused

No entity disambiguation (no Person / Organization markup on authors)

ArticleSchema uses author name string only — no sameAs URLs. Weakens E-E-A-T signals for AI retrieval

No speakable schema

speakable schema hints which text snippets are ideal for voice/AI extraction — missing entirely

RSS feed exists but rss.xml has no full content

Perplexity and other AI tools crawl RSS for freshness signals — include full article text in feed items
