## Red Flags & Course Corrections

🚩 **If GSC shows zero impressions after 2 weeks:**
- Check robots.txt still allows Google
- Verify pages are in XML sitemap
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console manually
- Check for 404 / redirect chains on affiliate links

🚩 **If Perplexity/ChatGPT never cite our FAQ:**
- Our answers may be too similar to Wikipedia/general sources
- Try making first sentence more unique and opinionated
- Check that answer length hasn't regressed (ensure 50+ words)

🚩 **If "v0 vs lovable" stays on Page 3+:**
- Check top-ranking competitors' Lovable page; improve EAT signals (author bio, credentials)
- Increase internal cross-linking to compare pages
- Consider outreach to AI dev newsletters to build backlinks
